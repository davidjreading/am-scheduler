Important caveats before you use this tool!

This is coded together by me as a non-expert and bugs exist, please tell me about them.
This is not policy, it’s a good-faith attempt at seeing what trying to comply with the contract might look like for the group which might be helpful as a tool for communication/bargaining.
Weeks and calls are minimums, people can exchange/give away weeks and ask for more weeks as long as contract hours allow
We’ve had to make a lot of assumptions about what we think an equitable way of distributing weeks is while doing out best to allow people to self-determine how much work they can meaningfully contribute. You can see some of these assumptions in the “Scheduling Rules” section on the bottom left.
“All Caps” means everyone chooses how much they work. “Senior Caps” means only members working for three years or longer can self-determine minimum work. “No Caps” means that nobody can. Nobody is advocating for anyone to work more than they want to or reasonably can, it’s just a demonstration of how call expectations and coverage change with these rules in place.
Even if RJH Second Doc is turned off, people can still voluntarily do second doc weeks. If it’s turned on it means we plan to schedule in a second doc every week (though not necessarily every day that week, just as much as the billing hours allow)
